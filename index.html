Publications & Projects

ResearchArcade: A Graph-Based Interface for Multi-Source Academic Data

Chongshan Lin

https://www.arxiv.org/abs/2511.22036

A comprehensive graph-based system unifying multi-source academic data from ArXiv and OpenReview. Led the construction of a large-scale ArXiv dataset with ~40,000 papers, 5M paragraphs, 500K figures, and 200K tables. Achieved significant performance improvements: 7.7% in figure/table insertion and 67% in revision retrieval tasks.
